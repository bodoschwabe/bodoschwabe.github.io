I am a postgraduate research assistant at the instute for astrophysics at Göttingen University. My research is focused on dark matter from both a cosmological and particle physics point of view. Currently, I investigate axionic dark matter using large scale simulations on high performance computing clusters. I aim on gaining a deeper understanding of axion dymanics on cosmological scales and on restricting the axion parameter space by comparing my numerical results to observational data.
